FACILITIES





BANK

The facility helps students to manage their money easily and conveniently by offering various benefits and discounts.It also include ATMs.It is one remarkable feature.

CLEAN DRINKING WATER

Quality of water is very good through out all the hostels.There is proper provision of pure treated safe drinking water.
No worries for that!!

COMPUTER LAB

It provides the students with a dedicated space to work on academic tasks,support collaborative work,and have extended operating hours, ensuring students have convenient access to technology for their studies.

DISPENSARY

The dispensary used as a convenient and accessible healthcare facility for the residents, providing basic medical services, advice, and medications to ensure their well-being during their stay in the hostel.

 

AMBULANCE

The ambulance service as essential for quick and efficient emergency response, timely transportation to hospitals, and ensuring the well-being of students in critical situations.

GYM

The gym serves as a dedicated space for physical fitness activities, offering health benefits, stress relief, convenience, and a sense of community.

BEAUTY PARLOUR

The beauty parlour offers a range of services for haircare, skincare, nails, makeup, relaxation, and self-expression. They will visit twice or thrice in a week.

TAILOR

The tailor provides clothing alteration and sewing services.They will visit twice or thrice in a week.








ELECTRICITY

The hostels have 24 hours power supply. Generators are used during power shutdowns. Well-trained electrician and plumber.

LAUNDRY

The laundry service is available and students can use it for low cost.
The clothes always come back perfect.

MESS

The hostel mess has a separate dining hall and a well-equipped kitchen catering. Mess serves breakfast, lunch, evening tea and dinner every day and the weekly menu is available with the mess in-charge.

WI-FI

Hostel provides FREE Wi-Fi.Students can access the internet in any area of the hostle with free high-speed Wi-Fi.
